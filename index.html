Financial literacy
Dive deep into the world of personal finance! This is your one-stop solution to get information on some commonly used terms that you will encounter in your daily life. This section is ideal for people who are starting from scratch and are looking for some quick guidance to begin their fin-educational journey.
PortFolio Income
Investing can be a tough nut to crack, especially after 2 long years of unwarranted optimism and glut of money in the markets. But don’t worry! We got you covered. Skim through this section to learn about different financial securities that might prove to be valuable assets to you.
Wealth Creation
Looking for ways to diversify your sources of income? Start here by learning about the various income sources you might want to adopt to supplement your job. Here, we will talk about freelancing, entrepreneurship and passive income, which can prove to be essential for maintaining a stable income in a volatile post-pandemic world.
Economy
The post-pandemic media is bombarding us with esoteric terms like inflation, fiscal deficit and free market economy to describe the economic institutions that govern your life. Take the matter in your hands and start learning about how the economy works by starting here.
Tools
We have made an encyclopedia listing some commonly used terms in the world of economics and finance. There is also a calculator for computing your accumulated sum of money over a period of time, assuming a constant growth rate. We will add more such useful tools in the future.
